About Box Boys

Box Boys is a local cardboard collection service created to make getting rid of unwanted boxes simple and convenient.

Cardboard can pile up quickly after moving, ordering packages, renovating, or running a business. We help clear that clutter by collecting cardboard directly from homes and businesses.

Our goal is to provide dependable service, simple scheduling, and responsible cardboard recycling.
